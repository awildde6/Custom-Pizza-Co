What To Expect at Custom Pizza Co

When you walk into Custom Pizza Co one of our Pizza Masters will greet you and guide you through the process.

  • You start with picking out your crust: thick, thin, gluten free, cauliflower, or no crust.
  • Next pick out your sauce: tomatoe, alfredo, BBQ, Buffalo and more.
  • Once you pick out your sauce you get to load you pizza with cheese! We have a varity of cheeses inclduing a vegan option.
  • Now its time for the toppings! We have so many toppings to choose from.
  • Lastly our Pizza Master will place your masterpiece into the over and we will call you when its finished.
